ABOUT SMVEC


Sri Manakula Vinayagar Engineering College was established in the year 1999, which is located in a sprawling green campus of 125 acres, possessing all the state-of- the art infrastructure facilities with 23 years of academic excellence, UGC has granted Autonomous Status to the institution on September 2019 and it was approved by Pondicherry Central University. The standard of quality imparted by the institution is ascertained by the institution level accreditation from NAAC with "A" grade and all undergraduate and MBA programmes are accredited by NBA, New Delhi. In addition to its glory, IT Giant Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) also accredited the institution. With the facilitation of the learning hub, the students of the institution have accomplished 118 University Gold Medals and 703 top ten-university ranks of Pondicherry University since 2003. Because of effective placement initiatives 90% of students are placed in reputed MNCs for the past decade and more than 1015 students are placed in the academic year 2020. The institution is offering 15 Under Graduate, 8 Post Graduate and 11 Doctoral Programmes. To provide skill based learning, the institution has established 17 Centre of Excellence to offer more than 90 skill oriented associate level International Certification Courses which are highly demandable and expectations of industries.


OBJECTIVE OF ICEITE' 22


The primary objective of the conference is to promote research and innovation in Recent Advances in Engineering and Technology. Also, the focal intention is to provide a platform for professionals involved in Engineering and Technology to exchange knowledge and gain cognizance in current technologies, techniques and solutions in the respective fields globally. Participants include People from research, academia, industrial sectors as well as government organizations. In addition to this, forum will help the delegates to establish or widen research, business relations, and collaborations both nationally and internationally in future..
