SUBMISSION GUIDELINES



• Full length research contribution, not submitted to any other journal/conferences, in word format as per the guide lines given in the Paper submission Template given in the conference website

• The abstract should be limited with 250 words and length of paper must be within six pages.

• Please mail your original manuscript mentioning your name, contact details and e-mail ids toiceite22_track1@smvec.ac.in,iceite22_track2@smvec.ac.in,iceite22_track3@smvec.ac.in

• All submitted papers will be checked for plagiarism and blind reviewed, the papers which are not satisfying the criteria will be summarily rejected.

• Notification of acceptance will be through email.

• All the accepted papers will be published in UGC approved journals and selected papers will be published in Scopus, & Web of Science indexed journals with the payment Prescribed by the Publisher.

• Registration will be confirmed only after the payment of registration fee and at least one author must be present for the conference.
